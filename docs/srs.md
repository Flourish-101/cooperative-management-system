# Software Requirements Specification (SRS)

## Cooperative Management Information System (CMIS)

**Version:** 1.0

**Prepared By:** Flourish Tops

**Date:** July 2026

---

This document specifies the functional and non-functional requirements for the Cooperative Management Information System (CMIS).

The purpose of the system is to digitize and streamline the operations of a cooperative society, including member management, contributions, attendance, loan processing, and reporting.

> **Note:** This document will be updated throughout the development lifecycle as requirements evolve.
